Bill Gates and Mms

2015-03-27
blog

During my time as a personal assistant to Yanis Varoufakis I collected information on the economic power that rules the world.

One day before a state meeting, I receive a brief about how to improve the greek economy. In that brief, there was a list of the most significant mining investments in 2010.

Tech companies were the biggest ones, and all of them had an interest in the congo. Most of these companies already stop mining there because of all the constant child abuse common in those countries.

At the time, I didn’t think about it, but a later visit, I was surprised about finding The Bill and Melinda foundation on the list. Why there were they on the list? Were they not suppose to be saving children?

Tracing the money back to its sources, I was able to find a small mine. This mine had not theoretical value, but Bill and Melinda were interested.

If you follow the money, you can see how the funds produced from that mine, when on selling MMS across the globe, probably increasing the Bill and Melinda pockets.

Will Bill invest in MMS?
